Password Validation - 
- pattern="(?=.*\d)(?=.*[a-z])(?=.*[A-Z]).{8,}"
1. contains at least one digit (\d)
2. contains at least one lowercase letter ([a-z])
3. contains at least one uppercase letter ([A-Z])
4. is at least 8 characters long (.{8,})
